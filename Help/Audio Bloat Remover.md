# Is Audio Bloat Remover Recommended?
> [!CAUTION]
> No Audio Bloat Remover isn't **recommended** as usage of the Audio Bloat Remover may result in sound or audio lose. 
> E.g. If you have Realtek audio drivers and you're device solely relies on them, it might not be the best idea to run it.

# Revert 
There is no revert method offered as Audio Bloat Remover removes the Services, and exe's of Realtek, Sound Research and VisiSonics.
So it would be better going to you're motherboard site and redownloading the audio drivers there!
